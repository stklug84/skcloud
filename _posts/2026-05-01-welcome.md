This is a small home for things I write, build, and teach as a cloud architect.

You'll find:

- **Blog posts** — short, opinionated notes from real projects.
- **Projects** — selected work I'm proud of (or learned the most from).
- **Architectures & Diagrams** — reusable references for cloud and platform design.
- **Books & Trainings** — what I read and recommend.
- **CV** — a scrollable timeline of my career.

The goal is simple: write things down so they're easier to share — and
easier to revisit later.
